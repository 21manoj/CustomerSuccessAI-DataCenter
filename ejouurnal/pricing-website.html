Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from other wellness apps?

Most apps just track. We connect the dots. You'll discover patterns like "social media drains you 16 points" or "your sleep threshold is exactly 6.5 hours" - backed by YOUR data, not population averages. This is self-knowledge, not just tracking.

What's the "Fulfillment Lineage"?

It's how we show connections between your choices. See how yesterday's walk created today's mental clarity (+12 points). How sleep affects focus. How movement creates calm. Your personal cause-and-effect map.

How long do check-ins take?

Under 20 seconds each. 3 steps: Pick mood (auto-advances), add context (optional), log micro-act (optional). We respect your time. 4√ó daily = 60 seconds total.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All data is end-to-end encrypted. Even we can't read it. Local-first architecture. Cloud backup is optional and zero-knowledge. You can export or delete everything anytime. GDPR compliant.

What's Premium+ for?

Premium+ is for therapists, coaches, and power users who want: guided programs, coach summaries (shareable with therapist), focus toolkit (app blocking), and API access. Most users are happy with Premium.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Cancel anytime, no commitments. Your data stays on your device. You keep access to free features. Export your data before canceling if you want.

Do you offer student/low-income discounts?

Yes! Email support@fulfillmentapp.com with your .edu email or proof of need. We offer 50% off Premium for students and those experiencing financial hardship. Everyone deserves self-knowledge.
