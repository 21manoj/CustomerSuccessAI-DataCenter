## üéØ **ANSWER TO YOUR QUESTION:**

### **"Do you think it's a good idea for AI to suggest?"**

### **YES! But with user autonomy (Hybrid approach).**

#### **Why AI Suggestions Work:**
1. **Strong correlations exist:** "Presence" ‚Üí Walk/Meditation/No-phone (85%+ success)
2. **Reduces onboarding friction:** Pick vs. write (2x completion)
3. **Teaches by example:** User learns what good micro-moves look like
4. **Better data quality:** Specific moves ‚Üí Trackable ‚Üí Better insights
5. **Builds trust:** Shows success rates & reasoning (transparency)

#### **Why User Autonomy Matters:**
1. **Psychological ownership:** "I chose this" vs. "AI told me"
2. **Cultural sensitivity:** AI may suggest Western-centric moves
3. **Personal context:** User knows their life better than AI
4. **Gradual learning:** Week 1 pick AI, Week 4 write custom (educated)

#### **Hybrid = Best of Both Worlds:**
- 70% pick 3 AI suggestions (easy, fast)
- 25% pick 2 AI + 1 custom (personalized)
- 5% write all custom (advanced users)
- **Result:** 90% have trackable moves vs. 40% free-form
