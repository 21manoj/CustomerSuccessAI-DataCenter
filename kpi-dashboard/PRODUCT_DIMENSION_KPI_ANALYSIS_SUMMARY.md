# Product Dimension KPI Impact Analysis - Summary

## Overview

Analyzed **68 KPIs** across 5 categories to identify which ones would benefit from SKU-based product dimension tracking for:
1. **Product Adoption**
2. **Support**
3. **Cross-sell/Upsell Opportunities**

## Key Findings

### Impact Distribution

- **High Impact (SKU Required)**: 17 KPIs (25%)
- **Medium Impact (SKU Recommended)**: 12 KPIs (18%)
- **Low Impact (Optional)**: 39 KPIs (57%)

**Total SKU Tracking Required**: 29 KPIs (43%)

## Implementation Recommendation

### Phase 1: Critical SKU Tracking (17 KPIs)
Implement product dimension for high-impact KPIs:
- All Product Usage high-impact KPIs (8)
- All Support high-impact KPIs (4)
- All Revenue high-impact KPIs (5)

### Phase 2: Enhanced SKU Tracking (12 KPIs)
Add SKU tracking for medium-impact KPIs based on data availability.

### Phase 3: Optional SKU Tracking
Consider product-specific tracking for sentiment KPIs if customer surveys include product-level questions.
